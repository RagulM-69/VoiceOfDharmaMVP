# Voice of Dharma Foundation

**Voice of Dharma Foundation** is a dharmic service organisation rooted in devotion to Hari and inspired by the timeless teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. The foundation was established to bring the wisdom of dharma closer to everyday life — not merely as philosophy, but as a living experience.

---

## About the Foundation

The foundation emerges from a simple recognition — that human life finds its highest meaning when aligned with truth, responsibility, and devotion. At the heart of its work lie three paths:

- **Karma Yog** — Selfless service, community support, and righteous action in daily life
- **Bhakti Yog** — Devotion to the divine, preservation of devotional culture and sacred practice
- **Gyaan Yog** — The pursuit of knowledge, spiritual education, and the study of scripture

Through these three paths, Voice of Dharma aspires to cultivate a way of life where action becomes service, knowledge becomes clarity, and devotion becomes a source of strength.

---

## Our Mission

To serve as a medium through which the principles of dharma can be understood, lived, and shared — by encouraging selfless action, sincere devotion, and the pursuit of true knowledge, so that individuals may lead lives of balance, purpose, and inner fulfilment.

---

## Donations & Support

The foundation accepts charitable donations to support its dharmic initiatives including:

- Community food service (Annadhanam)
- Preservation of devotional and cultural practices
- Spiritual education and dharmic outreach

> 💛 *The thought of donation itself is a great act of dharma.* Secure online donations via Razorpay will be enabled shortly, pending legal and registration formalities.

All donations are transparently managed and tracked through the foundation's internal administration system.
